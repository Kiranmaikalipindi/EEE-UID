Introduction

Smart homes integrate AI and IoT to improve efficiency, security, and convenience. Intelli Domotics leverages these technologies to automate tasks, optimize energy use, and enhance safety.

Why Intelli Domotics?

Traditional automation systems follow fixed rules, leading to inefficiencies. AI-powered automation adapts to user behavior and environmental conditions, ensuring better energy savings and security.

Key Features

  • Energy Optimization: AI minimizes power consumption.
  • Smart Security: Detects unusual activity and enhances safety.
  • Adaptive Automation: Adjusts lighting, temperature, and appliances.
  • Remote Monitoring: Allows control from anywhere via IoT.
  • Learning System: AI improves automation based on user habits.

With AI-driven intelligence and IoT connectivity, Intelli Domotics provides an efficient, scalable, and cost-effective smart home solution

Research Gap

  • Current smart home systems lack real-time adaptability based on user behavior.
  • Ineffieciencies in energy management.
  • Inefficiencies in current priority-based scheduling of appliances.
  • Need for improved IoT-based monitoring and predictive analytics.
  • Absence of AI-driven security threat detection.

    • Problem Statement

    Traditional smart home automation systems rely on predefined rules, leading to inefficiencies in energy consumption and security management. Intelli Domotics bridges this gap by integrating AI-driven adaptability, predictive analytics, and real-time decision-making to optimize appliance control and security monitoring.

    Objectives

  • Develop an AI-driven smart home automation system using ESP-32: This system will utilize the ESP-32 microcontroller for real-time control and automation of household appliances, ensuring seamless communication between devices and efficient energy management.
  • Integrate real-time learning to adjust device scheduling dynamically: By implementing machine learning algorithms, the system will analyze user habits and environmental factors to adjust appliance usage dynamically, reducing manual intervention and energy wastage.
  • Provide remote monitoring and control via IoT: An IoT-enabled platform will allow users to monitor and control home appliances remotely, providing real-time status updates, notifications, and an intuitive interface for seamless interaction.
  • Implement AI-based predictive analytics for better energy utilization: The system will use AI-driven data analysis to predict optimal energy usage, automate device control based on consumption patterns, and suggest energy-saving strategies to enhance efficiency and reduce costs.

    Methodology

    1. Garage Automation:

      • Vehicle Detection: IR sensor module.
      • Obstacle Safety: IR sensor to detect objects during shutter closure.
      • Relay Module: Controls shutter motor and lighting.

    2. Room 1 (Beside Garage):

      • Object Detection: PIR motion sensor for automatic door access.
      • Light Control:LDR sensor for ambient light adjustment.

    3. Room 2 (Above Room 1):

      • AI-Driven Fan Speed Control: Potentiometer for manual adjustment and DHT11/DHT22 for automatic speed modulation.

    4. Garden Automation:

      • Soil Moisture Monitoring: Smart soil moisture sensor for automated irrigation.
      • Water Pump: AI-controlled 5V submersible pump for optimized watering schedules.

    5. IoT & Connectivity:

      • MQTT (Message Queuing Telemetry Transport)-based real-time monitoring and remote access.
      • Cloud-based AI analytics for enhanced automation adaptability.

    6. AI Integration in Automation:

      • Machine Learning for Predictive Analysis: AI algorithms analyze past data to predict optimal appliance usage and reduce energy waste.
      • Adaptive Security: AI-enabled surveillance learns movement patterns and detects anomalies, triggering alerts.
      • Smart Energy Optimization:AI continuously learns household patterns and adjusts appliance schedules to maximize energy efficiency.
      • Real-Time AI Decision Making: AI dynamically prioritizes tasks based on user behavior and environmental changes.

    Conclusion

    The Intelli Domotics project is currently in the development phase, focusing on implementing AI-driven smart home automation with IoT integration. The primary objective is to enhance energy efficiency, security, and intelligent automation scheduling. The project involves designing the system architecture, selecting components, and testing core functionalities to ensure seamless integration and optimized performance. Through real-time automation and AI-based decision-making, the system aims to provide a more efficient, secure, and adaptive smart home experience.

    Future Scope

    The future scope of Intelli Domotics is centered around making the system more intelligent, adaptable, and scalable. With advancements in AI automation, energy optimization, and security, Intelli Domotics can evolve into a fully autonomous home automation system that offers maximum efficiency, security, and convenience to users.
    By continuously improving AI learning, integrating more smart devices, and enhancing cloud-based insights, the project can revolutionize how smart homes operate, paving the way for a more sustainable and automated future.
